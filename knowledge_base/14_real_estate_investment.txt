## **I. Introduction to Real Estate Markets**
Real estate investments generate **cash flow & long-term appreciation.**

- **Residential Properties:** Apartments, rental houses.
- **Commercial Real Estate (CRE):** Offices, malls, warehouses.
- **Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITs):** Publicly traded real estate funds.

## **II. Key Real Estate Metrics**
- **Capitalization Rate (Cap Rate):** Measures return on investment.

\[
Cap Rate = \frac{Net Operating Income}{Property Value}
\]

- **Gross Rental Yield:** Rental income vs. property price.

## **III. Example:**
- **Blackstone REIT** is the **largest real estate fund globally ($280B AUM).**
