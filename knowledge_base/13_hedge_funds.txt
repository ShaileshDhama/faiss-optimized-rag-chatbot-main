## **I. What Are Hedge Funds?**
Hedge funds use **aggressive investment strategies** to maximize returns.

- **Market-Neutral:** Balances long & short positions.
- **Global Macro:** Bets on large economic shifts (e.g., inflation trends).
- **Event-Driven:** Arbitrages corporate events (e.g., M&A deals).
