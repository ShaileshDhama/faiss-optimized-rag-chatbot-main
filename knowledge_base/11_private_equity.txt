## **I. Introduction to Private Equity**
Private equity (PE) involves investments in **privately held companies** rather than publicly traded firms.

- **Venture Capital (VC):** Invests in early-stage startups with high growth potential.
- **Growth Equity:** Funds mature startups looking to scale.
- **Leveraged Buyouts (LBOs):** PE firms acquire companies using **debt financing**.
