## **I. Introduction to Crypto Assets**
Cryptocurrencies are **decentralized digital assets** powered by **blockchain technology**.

- **Bitcoin (BTC):** First cryptocurrency, store of value.
- **Ethereum (ETH):** Smart contracts & decentralized apps (dApps).
- **Stablecoins (USDT, USDC):** Pegged to fiat currencies.
- **Non-Fungible Tokens (NFTs):** Unique digital ownership assets.
