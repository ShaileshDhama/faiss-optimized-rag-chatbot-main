# 🏛 FINANCIAL REGULATIONS & COMPLIANCE

## **I. Basel III Framework**
- **Capital Requirements:** Banks must hold **Tier 1 capital ≥ 6%**.
- **Liquidity Coverage Ratio (LCR):** Banks need **enough cash for 30 days**.

## **II. U.S. SEC Regulations**
- **Securities Act (1933):** Requires company financial disclosures.
- **Dodd-Frank Act (2010):** Prevents banking collapse.

## **III. Risk Management in Finance**
- **AML (Anti-Money Laundering):** Prevents illicit transactions.
- **KYC (Know Your Customer):** Verifies client identity.

📉 **Example:** In 2023, the SEC fined **Goldman Sachs $3B** for compliance failures.
