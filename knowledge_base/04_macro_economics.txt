## **I. Gross Domestic Product (GDP)**

\[

## **II. Inflation**
Measured using **Consumer Price Index (CPI)**.
- Central banks target **2% inflation** for stability.

## **III. Business Cycles**
- **Expansion:** GDP grows, low unemployment.
- **Recession:** GDP shrinks, high unemployment.
- **Recovery:** Economy rebounds.
