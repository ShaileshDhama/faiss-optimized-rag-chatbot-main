## **I. Value at Risk (VaR)**
Estimates maximum portfolio loss at a given confidence level.

\[
VaR = (Mean Return - Z \times Standard Deviation) \times Portfolio Value
\]

## **II. Portfolio Diversification**
- Combines **stocks, bonds, real estate, commodities** to reduce risk.

## **III. Stress Testing**
- Simulates **financial crises, interest rate shocks, or market crashes** to evaluate portfolio risk.
