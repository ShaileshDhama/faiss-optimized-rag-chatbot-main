## **I. Introduction to Insurance Markets**
Insurance manages **risk through pooled contributions**.

- **Life Insurance:** Term, Whole Life, Universal Life.
- **Health Insurance:** HMO, PPO, Medicaid, Medicare.
- **Property & Casualty (P&C):** Homeowners, Auto, Commercial.

## **II. Actuarial Science in Insurance**
Actuaries use **probability models & risk assessments**:

\[
Premium = Risk Factor \times Expected Loss + Loading
\]

- **Reinsurance:** Insurers transfer part of the risk to other insurers.

## **III. Example:**
- **AIG (American International Group)** nearly collapsed in **2008 due to excessive exposure to Credit Default Swaps (CDS).**
